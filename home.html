Make an access to information or personal information request

Discover and request federal government information that is available to the general public.

All government records (which include corporate documents and personal information) may be requested under the Access to Information Act.

Personal information about individuals held by a government institution may be requested under the Privacy Act.

Overview of request process

To make your request online, you will need to provide:

  1. the type of request you wish to make,
  2. details of your request,
  3. supporting documents (if applicable),
  4. your contact information, and
  5. a $5 processing fee (for Access to Information requests only).
